The new hybrid Arcade in town!

We are a Admission Based Arcade meaning No tokens or credits are required here. After paying the entry fee you are free to use any of our Arcade cabinets, Gaming Computers or Consoles. Play with your friends online or create some custom games on a local server. You can decide!

Pricing

We Focus on being a great hang out spot at an affordable price! We have 3 different Price points to accomadate your needs!

$7 for 2 hours

Our most affordable price point, great if you only have a few hours to spare.

$15 for all day

Best value, Play games all day! you are free to exit the arcade and return as you please.

New option! Monthly pass $70

Unlimited access to the arcade the whole month! come in any day during normal business hours and Play whatever you desire. You will also receive Tournament and Game request priorities!

Weekly SSBU Tournaments

We host weekly Super Smash brothers Ultimate Tournaments. $10 entry fee and 1st, 2nd and 3rd will take 60% of the money made in entry fees. Bring your setup(switch,dock,power cable and gamecube adapter) And you will receive a $5 dicount from your entry fee
